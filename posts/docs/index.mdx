# Introduction

Chirpy is an open source SaaS app that aims to provide a privacy-friendly and customizable comment system.

## Privacy

Chirpy takes privacy very seriously. We'll never track you and your users, and we won't sell or share your data with any third party companies.

## Customizable

Many comment systems are inflexible and difficult to customize. Chirpy is designed to be customizable and easy to use, it is easy to customize the look and feel of widgets to adapt the design system of your website, so it feels beautiful and intuitive. You can try it out right now! Just create a project and click the theme button on dashboard page after you sign in.
